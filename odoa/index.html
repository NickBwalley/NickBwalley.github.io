Welcome to
ODOA ORPHAN'S CARE AFRICA
(OOCA)

Providing a stable and consistent environment for the Poor and Fatherless Children

We ensure proper nutrition and health care provided to children

Services We Offer!..

We take care of 21 orphans under our residents.

We give them food, shelter and also good Education.

Furthermore, We have 30 more kids that we sponsor with their education with full tuition fees and also provide them with scholastic materials like: Books, pens, uniforms and other necessary needs

Mission and Vision

We offer home and Education for needy children and orphans without families...
We support a suitable training opportunities for children and young adults, to enable them to make a better future..

Our Portfolio

Our main aim is to give hope to the orphans despite they are disadvantaged due to lack of support: For Example
Odoa Hamidu a 23years old, a total orphan who is struggled with life and after all making it,
now he is helping kids who are less priviledged in the society..
Odoa Hamidu currently holds a diploma in education and currently practicing teaching as a job.. Out of his savings and with support from local partners he manages to look after the orphans in the society...
